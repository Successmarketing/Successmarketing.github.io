About Success Marketing

Our Journey Since 1991

Success Marketing was established in 1991 by Mr. Rajesh Agarwal, a pioneer in trading disposable items in Rajasthan, India.

Today, Success Marketing has grown to become one of the largest disposable product wholesalers in India. We are the proud leader in trading disposable plastic cups, glasses, containers, sustainable packaging, wrapping, and food containers ranging from 20ml to 2000ml.

Comprehensive Product Range

We deal in a wide variety of food packaging solutions including:

  • Plastic cups and containers
  • Paper cups and napkins
  • Thermocol and eco-friendly biodegradable options
  • Disposable plates, spoons, and mugs
  • Traditional clay pottery products
  • Food packaging for restaurants and catering
  • Sweet and chocolate boxes
  • Custom printed containers for brands

Our specialization includes custom printing on containers and boxes for restaurants, hotels, resorts, and caterers.

Our Commitment

We provide complete and best-in-class plastic packaging solutions to the food and QSR (Quick Service Restaurant) industry in India. Our focus remains on quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

About Success Marketing

Our Journey Since 1991

Success Marketing was established in 1991 by Mr. Rajesh Agarwal, a visionary entrepreneur who pioneered the trading of disposable food packaging items in Rajasthan, India. With over three decades of experience, our company has built a reputation for reliability, quality, and exceptional service.

Today, Success Marketing has grown to become one of the largest disposable product wholesalers in India. We are the proud leader in trading disposable plastic cups, glasses, containers, sustainable packaging, wrapping, and food containers ranging from 20ml to 2000ml, serving thousands of businesses across the country.

Comprehensive Product Range

We offer a diverse selection of food packaging solutions to meet the needs of restaurants, hotels, caterers, and food businesses of all sizes:

  • Plastic cups and containers in various sizes
  • Paper cups, plates, and eco-friendly napkins
  • Biodegradable and compostable packaging options
  • Thermocol products for insulation and protection
  • Disposable cutlery, plates, spoons, and mugs
  • Traditional clay pottery products
  • Sweet boxes and chocolate packaging
  • Custom printed containers with your brand logo

Our specialty includes customized printing services on containers and boxes, perfect for restaurants, hotels, resorts, and caterers looking to enhance their brand presence.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

As industry leaders, we recognize our responsibility toward the environment. We are continuously expanding our range of eco-friendly and biodegradable packaging solutions to help our clients reduce their environmental footprint while maintaining quality and functionality.

Our Vision

We strive to provide complete and best-in-class packaging solutions to the food and QSR (Quick Service Restaurant) industry in India. Our focus remains on quality, sustainability, customer satisfaction, and timely delivery.

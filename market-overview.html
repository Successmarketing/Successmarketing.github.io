Market Size and Growth in India

The disposable food packaging market has seen remarkable growth in recent years, with a current valuation of approximately ₹45,000 crores (USD 6 billion) and is expected to grow at a CAGR of 16-18% over the next five years.

Key Growth Drivers in India:

Food Delivery Boom

Zomato and Swiggy processing over 1.5 million orders daily, driving packaging demand

Urbanization

35% of India's population now living in urban areas with changing food habits

QSR Expansion

Quick-service restaurants expanding to Tier 2 and 3 cities

Hygiene Awareness

Post-pandemic focus on food safety and hygiene

Market Challenges:

  • Price sensitivity in Tier 2/3 cities
  • Infrastructure limitations for recycling
  • Regional variations in food preferences
  • Competition from traditional packaging methods

Expert Tips for Shawarma Boxes Buyers

  • The foil lining is non-negotiable - plain butter paper soaks through and ruins the customer experience.
  • For UAE and Saudi shawarma shops, double-wrap in foil paper + kraft sleeve is the standard.
  • Print your logo on the kraft sleeve, not the butter paper - the sleeve stays clean; the paper gets greasy.
  • For delivery, a small window in the kraft box helps delivery riders identify the product without opening the bag.

Expert Tips for Pizza Boxes Buyers

  • Use ventilated corner holes on the box to release steam and keep the crust crisp during long Swiggy / Zomato runs.
  • For the UAE and Middle-East climate, specify food-grade moisture-resistant liners - ambient humidity and hot delivery bags soften unprotected corrugated.
  • USA importers usually ask for FDA 21 CFR food-contact compliance certificates before clearing cartons. We ship with the paperwork by default.
  • For export, opt for pre-creased flat-pack format to maximise container fill and minimise freight cost per box.

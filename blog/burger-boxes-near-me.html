Expert Tips for Burger Boxes Buyers

  • Height is the silent killer of burger packaging - always leave 1 cm of clearance above the tallest burger on your menu.
  • Pair your burger box with a grease-proof paper square inside: it absorbs moisture, keeps the bun dry and costs only paise per piece.
  • For UAE and Saudi cloud kitchens, food-grade PP clamshells are preferred because of the heat during last-mile delivery.
  • For US exports, FDA-compliant bagasse clamshells are the fastest-moving SKU thanks to city-level SUP bans.

Expert Tips for Kulhad Cups Buyers

  • Soak kulhads in water for a few minutes before use to prevent heat cracks when hot chai is poured.
  • Stack kulhads with paper dividers during transport - clay on clay chips easily in transit.
  • For US / UK exports, pack kulhads in foam-padded cartons and declare them as 'terracotta earthenware food-contact cups'.
  • Kulhads elevate menu pricing - a Rs 8 paper chai becomes a Rs 30 kulhad chai with the exact same tea.

Expert Tips for Aluminium Foil Containers Buyers

  • Choose wrinkle-wall (corrugated side) foil for heavier gravy dishes - it resists denting during stacking.
  • For oven reheat, foil containers can go straight from fridge to oven up to 220°C - mention this on your takeaway label.
  • For export, pallet-wrap foil cartons tightly; loose foil dents in container shipping.
  • Multi-compartment trays reduce packaging spend by 20-30% on thali orders vs multiple single containers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are aluminium foil containers microwave safe?

Aluminium foil containers are NOT microwave safe - use them in an oven or for storage. If your customers microwave, shift to PP containers or remove the foil lid and transfer food to a microwave-safe plate.

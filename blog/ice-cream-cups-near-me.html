Expert Tips for Ice Cream Cups Buyers

  • Always use freezer-rated cups - regular paper cups become brittle at -18°C and crack under pressure.
  • Sundae cups with a dome lid create a photogenic visual that drives social-media shares.
  • For takeaway tubs, specify a tight-seal PP lid so melting ice cream doesn't leak in delivery bags.
  • Paired matching wooden spoons sit inside the lid - saves on separate cutlery packaging.

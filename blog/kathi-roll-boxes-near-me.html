Expert Tips for Kathi Roll Boxes Buyers

  • The foil-paper wrap goes directly around the roll; the kraft sleeve wraps the foil - this is the industry standard for clean branding.
  • For saucy rolls (schezwan chicken, chutney paneer), double-wrap foil paper at the bottom 2 inches to stop drip-through.
  • A small top opening in the kraft sleeve lets customers eat directly without unwrapping - important for food-truck service.
  • Printed kraft sleeves with a QR code to your online menu convert repeat orders at 2-3x the rate of unbranded packaging.

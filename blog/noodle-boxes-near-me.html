Expert Tips for Noodle Boxes Buyers

  • Noodle pails fold open into a plate - tell your customers and it becomes a delightful unboxing moment.
  • Keep soupy noodles (ramen, hakka soupy) in PP containers with tight lids - noodle pails leak soup.
  • Pack manchurian / curry separately in a PP tub and tuck inside the same delivery bag.
  • For export to US / UK Chinese restaurants, printed noodle pails with chopstick cutouts are a hit SKU.

Frequently Asked Questions

What size noodle box is standard in India?

26 oz (750 ml) is the best-seller for single-portion noodles and fried rice. 32 oz (1000 ml) is popular for sharing and family orders.

Are noodle boxes leak-proof for soupy dishes?

Paper noodle pails are designed for dry noodles and fried rice, not soupy dishes. For ramen, soup noodles and gravy, use a deep PP container with a tight snap-on lid.

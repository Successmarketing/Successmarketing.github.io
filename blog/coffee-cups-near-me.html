Expert Tips for Coffee Cups Buyers

  • Always pair hot cups with the correct lid thickness - a cheap lid on a premium cup is the most common cause of spillage complaints.
  • Ripple-wall cups photograph beautifully on Instagram - pay attention to colour consistency across batches.
  • For US cafe chains, 12 oz and 16 oz are the two best-selling sizes; for Indian cafes, 8 oz and 12 oz dominate.
  • Cold coffee cups must be PET or PP, not paper - condensation destroys paper cold cups.

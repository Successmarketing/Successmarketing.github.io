Expert Tip: Kachori And Samosa Packaging in Kota

Kachori grease saturates regular kraft boxes within 10-15 minutes. Always ask for food-grade grease-proof lining on your takeaway boxes - adds Rs 0.20-0.40 per box but saves every bag from bursting open on a scooter ride back to the hostel.

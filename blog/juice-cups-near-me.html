Expert Tips for Juice Cups Buyers

  • Use snap lids with a straw hole for takeaway; screw-cap bottles for cold-press delivery.
  • PET bottles need a food-grade tamper-evident seal for cold-press brands - customers expect it.
  • For detox / wellness brands, printed minimalist labels on clear PET cups outperform full-wrap stickers.
  • Paper cold cups are only cold-rated - don't use them for hot juices / masala chai.

Expert Tips for Salad Bowls Buyers

  • Use dome lids 30-40 mm tall so dressings and toppings don't crush in delivery.
  • Pack dressing separately in a 30-50 ml sauce cup with snap lid - never pre-toss a delivery salad.
  • Kraft bowls photograph warmer; PET bowls photograph sharper - choose per your brand identity.
  • For US and UK exports, bagasse bowls are the fastest-moving eco SKU for poke and grain bowl brands.

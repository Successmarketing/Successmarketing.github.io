Expert Tips for Cake Boxes Buyers

  • Always pair the box with a cake board (MDF or corrugated) slightly smaller than the inner dimension to keep the cake centred.
  • The window size matters - a 4" x 4" window on a 1 kg box gives the cleanest Instagram shot without compromising structure.
  • For exports and hot-climate markets, laminated window film is more reliable than thin PVC.
  • Stack packed cake boxes no more than three high during transport to avoid collapsing under cake weight.

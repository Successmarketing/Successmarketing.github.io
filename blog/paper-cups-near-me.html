Expert Tips for Paper Cups Buyers

  • For hot drinks above 65°C, specify at least 180 gsm paper with a food-grade PE or PLA lining - thin cups soak through within minutes.
  • Ripple-wall and double-wall cups eliminate the need for a sleeve, saving you Rs 1-2 per cup in consumables.
  • For US and EU exports, PLA-lined compostable cups are the fastest-moving SKU because of city-level single-use plastic bans.
  • For Indian chai stalls, 60-150 ml single-wall cups at Rs 0.30-0.70 per piece are the sweet spot.
